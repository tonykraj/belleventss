Complete Wedding Planning

PERFECT PARTNER FOR YOUR WEDDING

At Bell Events, We cover every aspect of  your wedding leaving nothing undone,From the concept to completion, we will work with you to plan a wedding that will reflect the unique personalities and lifestyle of our bride and groom.We will help you with location venues, designing your event, logistics, choosing prospective vendors, negotiating contracts and all the other details that goes into planning an event. We work towards shaping the  perfect wedding for you. We ‘ill orchestrate all the events beautifully so you and your loved one’s can enjoy the day without any hassle.

Venue Selection

We provide you with  all the information on all the venues, accommodation options, ceremony sites, and reception venues. Our dedicated wedding planning services has   expertise and experience when it comes to wedding planning .We  recommend venues  as per client preferences and ensure that you get the best rates.

Photography & Videography

Whether you’re having a simple or a grand wedding,  We provide you with expert photographers, who cover all pre-event and post-event ceremonies, apart from the main wedding. They capture those precious treasured moments of your wedding, Picturing candid and key moments that create intimate and beautiful moments. Together, we will create amazing   pictures of you and your family to enjoy for years to come.

Music and Entertainment

A wedding is incomplete without music, fun and entertainment .It provides fun and enjoyment. We provide you with the best of services in entertainment to create a memorable experience.
We provide Live music and Dance shows. DJ,  Fireworks, Puppet shows, Acrobat, Laser show, Sound and light shows, Entertainers, Live bands as per your preference .
We assist you with negotiation and getting the best deal

Wedding Design & Execution

Indian  weddings are  beautiful. With our  unique themes designed specially  for you, we assist you with the selection of wedding theme, design and décor.We creatively conceptualize your  wedding as per the requirements and budget. We assist in all requirements like menu selection, Mandap , center piece, floral decorations and much more.

Our Preferred Vendors

Logistic Management
